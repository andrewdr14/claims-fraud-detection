Motor Insurance Fraud Detection

Fraudulent insurance claims cost companies millions each year. This tool uses **machine learning** to analyze claims data and predict the **probability of fraud**, helping insurers make informed decisions.

How It Works:

  • 📊 Upload a **CSV** containing insurance claims data.
  • 🤖 The model analyzes key fraud indicators using **Random Forest Classifiers**.
  • ⚠️ It predicts whether a claim is **likely fraudulent or legitimate**.
  • 📁 Results are stored securely in **MongoDB Atlas** for further analysis.
